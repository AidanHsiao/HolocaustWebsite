Awards and decorations

Carl Spaatz earned a considerable amount of medals and awards during the war, 25 to be exact. A medal in the US Force was used to award either time served in the military, or a major accomplishment during a war. Spaatz ended up earning 25 of these medals, including ones for participating in the war, showing bravery during the war, and both World War victory medals.
